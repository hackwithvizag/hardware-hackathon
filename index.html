About Hardware Hackathon

Hardware Hackathon is a National Hackathon is conducted by Technical Club of Department of CSE of NSRIT in Collaboration with ECE, CSM and CSD. This event is sponsored under AICTE SPICES scheme. This high-stakes coding competition brings together the brightest minds in technology, challenging them to push their limits and create solutions that could shape the future.

With a team size of five members, participants form dream teams that blend diverse skills and perspectives. The clock starts ticking, and for the next 24 hours, these teams dive headfirst into the world of coding, designing, and strategizing. The goal? To develop innovative solutions to real-world problems that can disrupt industries and make a lasting impact.Join us on this exciting journey of innovation and creativity. Let's push the boundaries of what's possible and create solutions that make a positive impact on society.

Sustainable Energy Solutions

Solutions using renewable sources like solar and wind to reduce reliance on fossil fuels for cleaner, more sustainable energy.

Smart Healthcare Gadgets

Innovative devices integrating sensors and connectivity for real-time health monitoring and improved patient outcomes.

Environmental Monitoring and Protection:

Use of technology, such as sensors and data analytics, to monitor and protect the environment, including air and water quality.

Open Innovation

Joining the open-source movement fosters collaboration and knowledge-sharing. By contributing to open-source projects, you not only enhance your skills but also contribute to the collective progress of the community. It's a dynamic space where innovation thrives through shared efforts, creating a more inclusive and robust digital landscape.

Smart City Solutions with IoT and Drones

Utilization of IoT devices and drones to enhance urban living, optimize city operations, and improve services for a more efficient and sustainable cityscape.

Automation and Robotics in Everyday Life

Integration of intelligent machines and robots in daily activities for increased efficiency and convenience, from smart homes to autonomous devices.

Rules and Guidelines

  • This is a team participation hackathon. participants must form a team of 2 - 5 members.
  • The hackathon will start on January 5th, 2024, at 09:00 AM IST, and end on January 6th, 2024, at 10:00 AM IST, you work on your hack during the allotted time only. Older projects will not be allowed. We will require you to build your project on the hackathon weekend.
  • It's an in-person hackathon, you can participate from anywhere but have to come to the venue.
  • There are 5 themes of the hackathon, you must submit a hack that is in one of these themes.
  • It is mandatory for every team to be register google forms and join whatsapp group .
  • Hackers are not allowed to work on multiple teams and/or submit multiple projects. Hackers must be a part of only one team and submit only one project.
  • We will release the detailed problem statements on 2nd January 2024.
  • The first 20 teams to register will secure a spot in the event. However, for additional teams, on-the-spot registration will be considered only if their project ideas align with our requirements; otherwise, we regret that we won't be able to accommodate them.
